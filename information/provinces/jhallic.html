Jhallic Refuge

The Jhallic Refuge is the northernmost province of Bailecha, it is inhabited by a large ethnic minority of Jhallicans who were invited into the area by the Bailech king in response to the conquest of Jhallica, hence the name Jhallic Refuge. It is a very lightly populated region which has been a source of conflict between the Bailechs and Hulimics for many years.
