Velvon

Velvon is semi-autonomous Principality within the Bailech Empire. It is legally integrated into the empire in Personal Union with the Kingdom of Bailecha. It possesses a strong regional culture which is a blended mix of Bailech and Thelicean, the people there speak and mangeled Bailech-Thelicean language as well. Before entering Personal Union with Bailecha after the Great Affair the Principality was much more powerful power with almost double the territory.
